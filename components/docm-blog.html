Blog entry from a .docm (Word) document

This blog post was written in Word and saved as a .docm (macro-enabled) Word document. The document gets automatically converted into HTML by the WEIRD build process.

Supported

The .docm conversion supports pretty much anything thrown at it. OK, not necessarily anything. WEIRD hasn't been tested exhaustively yet. But it supports a lot.

Limitations

The conversion process for .docm files takes significantly longer than the one for .docx files. Stick to .docx files to the maximum extent possible. Use .docm in situations needing tables and other complex things.
